Licensing

Licensing is easy.

  1. Go to the backoffice
  2. Click on the Block Farm Editor section.
  3. Select your domain. If no domains are specified in umbraco, the current domain will be assumed.
  4. Click (Re)Validate

If you have any issues with licensing, please Contact Us.
